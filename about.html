About

brgsttr.de is intentionally minimal. It exists as a private contact endpoint and a small, curated reference surface.

What this site is

  • A place for short notes that are meant to be read as-is (no derivatives by default).

What this site is not

  • No tracking, no newsletter, no social integrations.
